    Why trusting Abyss Explore to organize your trip ?

    Trips for all levels divers !

    Abyss Explore is a travel agency specialize in diving stays. Our particularity, we adapt your trip according to your level. If you are a beginner, we will take care of finding supervisors to make you live the most beautiful divings. If you are experienced, we will only take care of fiding the place and the equipment, we let you take care of the rest !

    Travel alone, with friends or with family.

    At Abyss Explore, our trips are suitable for everyone. Pour loner travelers looking for an immersive experience, we propose solo divings. For friends groups wishing to enjoy of quiet mornings after animated nights, we organize afternoon divings. Finally, for families desiring to mix morning divings and afternoon tourism, we also personnalize your itineraries.

    Divings respecting the environment.

    Abyss Explore organize diving trips that preserve our precious environment. By working together with local partners engaged in the sustainable development, we are proud to protect the submarine while offering unforgettable experiences. By choosing Abyss Explore, we dive in beautiful waters while respecting submarine life
